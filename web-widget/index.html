AI Chatbot Widget — Demo Page

This page demonstrates the embeddable chatbot widget. Click the 💬 button in the bottom-right corner.

How to embed in any app

<script
  src="chatbot-widget.js"
  data-api-url="https://your-chatbot-api.run.app/api/chat"
  data-title="AI Assistant"
  data-theme="#1A73E8"
  defer
></script>

Sample questions to try

  • What does SLA mean?
  • Show me active critical events
  • What is the KPI report for this month?
  • Explain RBAC in our system context
  • What entities have SLA breaches?
